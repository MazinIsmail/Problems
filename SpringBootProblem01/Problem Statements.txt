Problem Statements:-

Problem 01:

Write a method in Java 8+, such that the method generates a sequence of numbers between 1 and 1000 inclusive.
It then keeps only the ones which are multiples of 3 or 5 and then squares them.
It returns the first 10 numbers from the series.

Problem 02:

Finds the indices of two numbers from the array whose sum will equal to the given target.

Problem 03:

Counts the number of words ending in 'y' or 'z' (not case sensitive).
'y' or 'z' is at the end of a word if there is not an alphabetic letter immediately following it.
